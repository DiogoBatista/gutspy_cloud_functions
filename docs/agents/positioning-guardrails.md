# GutSpy IBS 2.0 Agents Guide
## Category: Positioning Guardrails

### Purpose
Keep all product, marketing, and UX work aligned with the IBS-only pivot.

### Always true
- GutSpy is IBS-only and journaling-first.
- The app is not medical advice.
- Logging must be fast and low friction.

### Avoid these positioning traps
- Becoming a calorie tracker again
- Overpromising "AI recommendations"
- Claiming diagnosis or treatment

### Words to avoid
- calories, macros, weight loss, diet tracker (as primary)
- science-backed recommendations (sounds like medical claims)
- diagnose IBS
- cure

### Preferred language
- "Track" and "log"
- "Spot patterns"
- "May be associated with"
- "Based on your logs"
- "Seen X times in your logs" (instead of n=)

### UX guardrails
- Never block onboarding, first Bowel Movement (BM) log, timeline, basic weekly summary.
- Premium gates only: patterns detail, advanced trends, export report, reminders settings, 14-day mode.

### Output formats this category should produce
- Copy QA checklist
- Writing style guidance for screens and notifications
- Premium gating messaging rules
